Victoria Memorial

A magnificent white marble monument built in memory of Queen Victoria. It Reflects a blend of British and Mughal architectural styles and houses a museum displaying artifacts, paintings, and memorabilia from the colonial era.

Howrah Bridge

An iconic cantilever bridge connecting Kolkata with Howrah across the Hooghly River. It is an engineering marvel and one of the busiest bridges in the world.
Kalighata Temple

he Kalighat Temple is a significant and revered Hindu pilgrimage site located in Kolkata, West Bengal.
Indian Musuem

The Indian musuem was established in 1814, and is the oldest museum in India. It Features a vast collection of art, archaeology, anthropology, and natural history exhibits.
Birla Planetarium

The Birla Planetarium in Kolkata is a popular attraction for both locals and tourists alike. It is located in the heart of Kolkata, it is one of the largest planetariums in Asia and provides immersive shows on astronomy and space science.

Sundarbans National Park

Sundarbans National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the largest mangrove forests in the world. It is known for its Royal Bengal Tigers, crocodiles, and rich biodiversity.
